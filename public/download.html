What is CMO Meter OCR App?

CMO Meter OCR App is a powerful mobile application designed for utility meter reading and Change Meter Owner (CMO) management. Using advanced OCR (Optical Character Recognition) technology powered by Google ML Kit, the app automatically reads meter displays, eliminating manual data entry errors and increasing field worker productivity.

Built for field technicians and CMO processing officers, this app works completely offline, making it perfect for remote areas without internet connectivity.

Problem Manual meter reading leads to errors and slow data processing
Solution AI-powered OCR automatically reads meter displays with high accuracy
Problem Paper-based records get lost or damaged
Solution Digital forms with secure local database and cloud sync

User Guide

Learn how to use the app effectively

  • 1

    Download the APK

    Click the "Download APK" button above to download the latest version of the app to your device.

  • 2

    Enable Unknown Sources

    Go to Settings → Security → Unknown Sources and enable it to allow installation from sources other than Play Store.

    On newer Android versions, you may be prompted to allow installation when opening the APK.
  • 3

    Install the App

    Open the downloaded APK file and tap "Install". Wait for the installation to complete.

  • 4

    Grant Permissions

    When you first open the app, grant the required permissions:

    • Camera - For OCR and barcode scanning
    • Storage - For saving photos
    • Location - For geotagging (optional)
  • 1

    Register an Account

    On first launch, tap "Register" and fill in your details: Full Name, Email, and Password. Tap "Register" to create your account.

  • 2

    Login

    Enter your registered email and password, then tap "Login" to access the app.

  • 3

    Explore the Dashboard

    The home dashboard shows your statistics: total CMOs, pending uploads, and recent activities. Use the navigation to access different features.

  • 1

    Start New CMO

    From the dashboard, tap the "+" button or go to CMO menu and select "Add New CMO".

  • 2

    Enter Customer Information

    Fill in: Customer Name, Mobile Number, Email, NID, NOCS, and Address. Tap "Next" to continue.

  • 3

    Meter Information

    Enter or scan the Customer ID and New Meter ID using the barcode scanner. Use OCR to capture the meter reading automatically.

  • 4

    Old Meter & Seals

    Record old meter details, take photos, and enter all seal numbers (Battery Cover, Terminal A, B, C).

  • 5

    Save or Submit

    Choose "Save as Draft" to edit later, or "Submit" to mark as ready for upload.

  • Ensure Good Lighting

    Make sure the meter display is well-lit. Avoid shadows and use flash in dark conditions if needed.

  • Position Camera Correctly

    Hold your device parallel to the meter display at a distance of 15-30 cm. The display should fill most of the frame.

  • Focus Properly

    Wait for auto-focus to complete. Numbers should be sharp and clear. Tap the screen to refocus if needed.

  • Hold Steady

    Keep your hand steady when capturing. Wait for the green indicator before taking the shot.

  • Verify the Reading

    Always verify the OCR result before proceeding. Retake if the reading is incorrect.
